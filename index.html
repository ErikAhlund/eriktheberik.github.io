About Me



Having studied video game programming in one of the top universities for the field and worked for Ubisoft in the development of Assassin’s Creed Odyssey, I’ve learned that in software development, especially in game development, you’re going to do something you’ve never done before.

I learned to excel at this. When developing our first 3d game (using our own 3D engine) at university we decided to make a hover racer called Vertigo Rush (similar to Redout). The only problem was that we weren’t allowed to use a third-party physics engine, so I decided to develop my own physics engine in 1 week; I didn’t even know what a quaternion was before starting.
